# ⚡ Electricity Bill Calculator — India

A simple, clean web app to calculate your monthly electricity bill based on **state-wise slab rates** across India.

## 🌐 Live Demo
[Click here to view](https://arpan-creates.github.io/electricity-bill-calculator)

---

## ✨ Features

- 🗺️ Covers **17 Indian states** with real tariff slab rates
- 🏠 Supports **Domestic, Commercial & Industrial** consumer types
- 📊 Shows full **slab-wise energy charge breakdown**
- 💰 Includes **Fixed charge, Meter rent, Fuel surcharge & Tax**
- 📱 Mobile friendly & fully responsive
- 🌙 Dark themed UI

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Select your **state**
2. Choose **consumer type** (Domestic / Commercial / Industrial)
3. Enter **units consumed** (kWh)
4. Hit **Calculate Bill** ⚡

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

Rates are approximate and based on general tariff orders.
Actual bills may vary. Always check your DISCOM for exact rates.
